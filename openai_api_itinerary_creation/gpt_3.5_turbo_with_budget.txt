ay 1:
- Morning: Visit the Louvre Museum to explore world-renowned art pieces including the Mona Lisa. Pro tip: Pre-book tickets to skip the line.
- Lunch: Enjoy a traditional French meal at Le Saint Régis, a charming brasserie near Île de la Cité.
- Afternoon: Walk to Notre-Dame Cathedral to admire its stunning Gothic architecture and learn about its history.
- Evening: Stroll along the Seine River and have dinner at Le Procope, the oldest café in Paris.

Day 2:
- Morning: Start your day at Musée d'Orsay, a former railway station turned art museum featuring Impressionist masterpieces.
- Lunch: Indulge in a classic French dish like Coq au Vin at Chez André, a cozy bistro in the Saint-Germain-des-Prés area.
- Afternoon: Explore the historic Palace of Versailles, a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its opulent gardens and Hall of Mirrors.
- Evening: Head back to Paris and have dinner at L'Atelier de Joël Robuchon for a Michelin-starred dining experience.

Day 3:
- Morning: Visit the Centre Pompidou, a modern art museum with a unique architectural design and a vast collection of contemporary art.
- Lunch: Savor some delicious crepes at Breizh Café, a popular spot for authentic Breton cuisine.
- Afternoon: Take a leisurely walk through Montmartre neighborhood, visit Sacré-Cœur Basilica for panoramic views of the city.
- Evening: Enjoy a farewell dinner at Les Ombres, a rooftop restaurant offering a breathtaking view of the Eiffel Tower.

Expected Expense Total:
- Accommodation (3 nights in a mid-range hotel): $600
- Meals (3 days): $300
- Museum/Attraction tickets: $150
- Local transportation: $50
- Total: $1,100

Travel Tips:
- Consider purchasing a Paris Museum Pass for discounted entry to various museums and attractions.
- Use public transportation like the metro or buses to get around the city efficiently.
- Try to learn a few basic French phrases to enhance your interaction with locals.
- Stay aware of your belongings, especially in crowded tourist areas.
- Relax and take your time to soak in the beauty and culture of Paris!
